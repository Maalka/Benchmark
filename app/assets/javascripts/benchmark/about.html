Maalka and Architecture 2030 have developed the Energy Target and Rating Tool for building owners and 2030 members. This tool is used to effectively compare their energy-use intensity with similar building types, understand the change of their energy-use efficiency over time, and set energy-use intensity targets. Basing the tool functionality on the 2003 CBECS dataset enables 2030 members to maintain pre-existing goals and baselines so that progress can continue to be consistently measured.
